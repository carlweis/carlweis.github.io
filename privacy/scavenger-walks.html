Privacy Policy for Scavenger Walks

Effective Date: February 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Scavenger Walks ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data unless explicitly provided by the user. The app may collect:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve functionality.
  • User-Generated Content: Items added to scavenger hunts, stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To store scavenger hunt items locally for your convenience.
  • To provide optional features like progress tracking.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not store or transmit personal information to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) to understand app usage trends. These services may collect anonymized data.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
